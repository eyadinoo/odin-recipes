Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Our most popular banana bread is moist, delicious, and absolutely packed with banana flavor. This community favorite will quickly become your go-to banana bread recipe!

We hope you enjoy this delicious yet healthy dessert!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Method

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
